Which KEENON cleaning robot fits a hotel best?

The C30 suits compact areas, the C40 is usually the most balanced model for ROI, and the C55 is better for large surfaces or intensive use.

Yes. Alpha Robotics can estimate freed-up hours, cost per m², cleaning frequency and payback with an indicative ROI calculator.
