Frequently asked questions about the KEENON C30

The questions we get before deploying a KEENON C30. If you can't find yours, message us on WhatsApp.

How much does the KEENON C30 cleaning robot cost?

The C30 is offered as a RaaS model, with a fixed monthly fee and no upfront investment, or as a direct purchase (CAPEX) on request. The price depends on the configuration and the number of units; you can get an estimate in the configurator or request a free, no-obligation assessment.

What size of space is the C30 designed for?

The C30 is geared towards daily upkeep of compact and common areas —hotels, retail, offices and walkways— with vacuuming and mopping. For large facilities or intensive operations, the C40 and C55 models offer greater capacity.

Does the C30 clean carpeted floors or rugs?

The C30 works mainly on hard floors (marble, tile, stoneware, vinyl). On carpeted areas it can vacuum depending on configuration, and it can automatically exclude specific areas using the facility map.

What is the lead time for the C30?

The indicative lead time for delivery and deployment is a few weeks from order confirmation, depending on stock and configuration. The exact date is set during the assessment, which includes mapping and team training.

What installation requirements does the C30 need?

Minimum requirements: stable WiFi, a power socket for the charging dock, accessible floors with no impassable steps and an initial mapping of the space. We carry out a prior analysis of the installation before go-live.
