Frequently asked questions

How is the ROI of a cleaning robot calculated?

It compares the cost of manual cleaning (hours spent multiplied by labour cost) with the cost of the robot, whether purchased or rented, plus consumables and maintenance. Using your surface area, shifts and cost-per-hour data, the calculator estimates the monthly saving, the cost per square metre and the payback period.

What is the typical payback of a KEENON robot?

It depends on the surface area, the shifts and the labour cost of each facility. With intensive cleaning in hotels and large facilities it is usually reached in the first months of use; the calculator gives you the exact figure for your case.

Is it better to buy the robot or take it on renting (RaaS)?

A direct purchase pays off sooner in the long run; renting or RaaS (with Grenke, over 36 or 48 months) avoids the upfront investment and turns the robot into a monthly operating fee. The calculator compares both scenarios so you can decide with numbers.
