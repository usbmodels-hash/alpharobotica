What is the KEENON T11 robot used for in F&B?

The T11 is geared towards floor service, visual promotion, carrying plates and drinks, and experiences where the robot needs to add impact as well as efficiency.

Is the T11 suitable for hotels and events?

Capabilities, requirements and limitations

  • F&B T range: payload up to 40 kg depending on the model for floor service, buffet, clearing and kitchen-to-floor support, with visual communication on screen
  • Integration/connectivity: W3, S100 and the C range certified for multi-floor integration; autonomous use of lifts and automatic doors via a dedicated network and IoT (LoRaWAN/radio frequency), without interfering with guest WiFi.

Maintenance and SLA

  • Permanent technical support, parts, labour, technician call-outs and consumables included in the OPEX model.
  • In the event of a critical breakdown that compromises operations: replacement of the robot with a new or courtesy unit at no additional cost.

Frequently asked questions about the KEENON T11

The questions we get before deploying a KEENON T11. If you can't find yours, message us on WhatsApp.

How much does the KEENON T11 waiter robot cost?

The T11 is available as a RaaS model (fixed monthly fee, no upfront investment) or as a direct purchase on request. The price depends on the configuration and units; calculate an estimate in the configurator or request a free assessment.

What is the T11's screen for?

The T11 includes a screen for visual communication: promotions, welcome messages, brand content or service information, in addition to floor transport. It's useful when you want to use the robot as a communication channel too.

How much weight and battery life does the T11 have?

It carries up to 20 kg (5 kg per intermediate tray and 10 kg on the base), moves at 0.1-1 m/s and fits through aisles of just 49 cm. Its 18.5-inch screen makes it ideal for visual communication.

What is the lead time for the T11?

The indicative lead time is a few weeks from confirmation, depending on stock and configuration, and includes setting up on-screen content and training.

What installation requirements does the T11 need?

Stable WiFi, a power socket for the charging dock, aisles with enough room and an initial mapping of the dining room.
