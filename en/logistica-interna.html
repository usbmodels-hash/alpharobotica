SEO FAQ

Frequently asked questions about internal logistics.

What is the difference between the S100 and S300?

The S100 is geared towards medium loads and agile routes; the S300 is built for heavy loads and intensive logistics operations.

Can it work in hospitals or laundries?

Yes, as long as the assessment validates routes, loading points, aisle width, elevators, door interaction and internal protocols.
