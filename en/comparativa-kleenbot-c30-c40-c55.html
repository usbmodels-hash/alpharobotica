FAQ

Which model is the most balanced for hotels?

In most mid-size hotels, the KLEENBOT C40 is the sweet spot for coverage, tank and operating cost.

When should I choose the C55?

When the floor area per shift clearly exceeds 2,000 m² or there is heavy traffic and a need for high capacity.

Is the C30 good enough for hotels?

Yes, in small hotels, specific zones or compact spaces where a larger robot isn't worth installing.

How much does each model cost?

As a guide, the KLEENBOT C30 starts at €8,900, the C40 at €12,500 and the C55 at €18,900 (+ VAT). They are also available on a leasing (RaaS) plan with Grenke. The final price is on request depending on configuration and included services.
