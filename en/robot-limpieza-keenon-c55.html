C55 SEO FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the KEENON C55 before requesting a demo.

We answer the main questions to decide whether this autonomous cleaning robot for large facilities fits your hotel, restaurant, hospital, large facility or logistics flow.

When should you choose the KEENON C55 instead of the C40?

The C55 is designed for large facilities, shopping centres, hospitals, car parks, factories and spaces where cleaning volume and tank capacity matter more than maximum compactness.

What kind of business gets the most return from the C55?

The greatest return usually appears in operations with many square metres, long shifts, extensive indoor floors and a need to keep cleaning constant without increasing headcount.

Does the C55 require building work or floor modifications?

Deployment is validated during the assessment. In typical conditions, autonomous navigation avoids invasive solutions and allows maps, routes and work zones to be configured without major modifications.

Can Alpha Robotics compare the C40 and C55 in a single visit?

Yes. The assessment can compare the C40 and C55 based on floor area, obstacles, charging points, cleaning frequency, schedules and ROI targets.

Frequently asked questions about the KEENON C55

The questions we get before deploying a KEENON C55. If you can't find yours, message us on WhatsApp.

How much does the KEENON C55 cleaning robot cost?

The C55 is available as a RaaS model (fixed monthly fee, no upfront investment) or as a direct purchase on request. The price varies with configuration and units; calculate an estimate in the configurator or request a free assessment.

What area does the C55 cover?

The C55 is a high-capacity robot designed for large facilities and intensive operations: hospitals, shopping centres and industrial sites. For more compact areas, the C30 or C40 fit better.

How does the C55 differ from the C40?

The C55 prioritises capacity and battery life to cover more metres per cycle in demanding environments, while the C40 balances performance and size for hotels and mid-size facilities. You can see this in the C30, C40 and C55 comparison.

What is the lead time for the C55?

The indicative lead time for delivery and deployment is a few weeks from confirmation, depending on stock and configuration, and includes facility mapping and training. We confirm the exact date during the assessment.

What installation requirements does the C55 need?

It needs stable WiFi, a power socket for the charging dock, accessible hard floors and an initial mapping of the space. Before go-live we carry out an analysis of the installation.
