The data is processed to respond to information requests, prepare operational assessments, manage demos, send commercial proposals, handle queries and maintain communications related to KEENON robotics solutions.

The data will be kept for as long as necessary to handle the request and, where applicable, to maintain the commercial or pre-contractual relationship. When it is no longer necessary, it will be blocked for the applicable legal periods and subsequently deleted.
