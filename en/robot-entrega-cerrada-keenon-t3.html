T3 SEO FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the KEENON T3 before requesting a demo.

We answer the main questions to decide whether this hygienic enclosed delivery robot fits your hotel, restaurant, hospital, large facility or logistics flow.

What sets the KEENON T3 apart from an open waiter robot?

The DINERBOT T3 has an enclosed cabin to protect food, amenities or sensitive products, so it fits hospitals, hotels and operations that need more hygiene or privacy.

Is the T3 suitable for hospitals and care homes?

Yes. Its enclosed delivery design helps in flows where you want to protect the contents, reduce handling and keep service more controlled in healthcare or care settings.

Can the T3 be used for high-end room service?

Yes. In hotels, the T3 can complement the W3 when you need protected delivery, controlled access or transport of products that shouldn't travel on open trays.

What does Alpha Robotics review before installing a T3?

We review routes, aisle width, delivery points, product types, opening protocols, safety and metrics to justify the pilot.

Frequently asked questions about the KEENON T3

The questions we get before deploying a KEENON T3. If you can't find yours, message us on WhatsApp.

How much does the KEENON T3 enclosed delivery robot cost?

The T3 is offered as a RaaS model or a direct purchase on request. The price depends on the configuration and the number of units; get an estimate in the configurator or request a free assessment.

What kind of deliveries does the T3 make?

The T3 is designed for enclosed, protected deliveries: it moves items in a sheltered compartment, ideal for discreet room service, private deliveries or transport of items that shouldn't be left exposed.

How much weight does the T3 take and what is its battery life?

It takes up to 40 kg depending on configuration and offers up to 12 hours of battery with a 4-to-5-hour charge, enough for a full day of deliveries.

What is the lead time for the T3?

The indicative lead time for delivery and deployment is a few weeks from confirmation, depending on stock and configuration, and includes route setup and training.

What installation requirements does the T3 need?

Stable WiFi, a socket for the charging dock, accessible routes and an initial mapping. In multi-floor buildings it can integrate with compatible lifts; we assess this in the prior analysis.
