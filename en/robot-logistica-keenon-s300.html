Frequently asked questions about the KEENON S300

The questions we get before deploying a KEENON S300. If you can't find yours, message us on WhatsApp.

How much does the KEENON S300 logistics robot cost?

The S300 is offered as a RaaS model or a direct purchase on request. The price depends on the configuration and the number of units; get an estimate in the configurator or request a free, no-obligation assessment.

How much weight does the S300 carry?

The S300 is designed for heavy loads up to 300 kg, double the S100, for demanding logistics flows of internal transport between areas.

What is the difference between the S300 and the S100?

The main difference is load capacity: the S100 goes up to 150 kg and the S300 up to 300 kg. The choice depends on the usual weight of the loads and the type of logistics flow.

What battery life does the S300 have and what floors does it work on?

It offers up to 6 hours of battery with a 4-to-6-hour charge and operates on hard, even floors without raised obstacles, with LiDAR navigation and perimeter safety.

What installation requirements does the S300 need?

Stable WiFi, a socket for the charging dock, clear and accessible routes and an initial mapping. We validate the routes and loads in a prior analysis.
