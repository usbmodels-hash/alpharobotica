Frequently asked questions about Room Service.

Can the W3 use elevators?

Yes, through a validated integration with the E-Box module and always after reviewing the elevator's compatibility and the building's safety.

What can a room service robot deliver?

Amenities, bottles, internal orders, documents and private deliveries to the room or reception. Depending on the case, the W3, T3 or even the S100 is the right fit.
