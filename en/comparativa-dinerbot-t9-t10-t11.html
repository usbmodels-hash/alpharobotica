FAQ

Which model should I choose for a hotel buffet?

The T9 and the T10 are usually the best fit, depending on volume, brand image and the need for visual communication.

Which waiter robot is the most premium?

The T10 and the T11 are the models most geared towards visual experience and premium venues.

Do they work in narrow aisles?

Yes, but it's worth validating widths, turns and crossing points during the operational assessment.

How much does a DINERBOT waiter robot cost?

The price depends on the model (T9, T10 or T11) and the configuration. It is available to buy or on a leasing (RaaS) plan with Grenke as a monthly fee. Request a no-obligation quote for your restaurant.
