SEO FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Food & Beverage.

Which KEENON waiter robot is best for a buffet?

The T9 and T10 are usually good options for buffet and kitchen-to-table; the T8 is more compact, the T11 helps in narrow aisles and the T3 adds enclosed delivery when the contents need more protection.

Do waiter robots replace waiters?

No. They reduce repetitive trips and help staff spend more time on customer service, upselling and overseeing the service.
