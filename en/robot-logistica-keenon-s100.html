What is the KEENON S100 used for in internal logistics?

The S100 automates internal transport of linen, boxes, restocking, material, warehouse, laundry, stores and back-of-house in hotels, hospitals and large facilities.

What sets the S100 apart from a small service robot?

The S100 is designed for loads and logistics flows, not just visible service. It's useful when the main problem is internal trips and moving material.

Integration can be assessed during the assessment. Alpha Robotics reviews lifts, doors, routes, loading points and safety before proposing the solution.

Capabilities, requirements and limitations

  • S100 logistics: back-of-house support, linen, stores and internal flows with certified multi-floor integration.
  • Integration/connectivity: W3, S100 and the C range certified for multi-floor integration; autonomous use of lifts and automatic doors via a dedicated network and IoT (LoRaWAN/radio frequency), without interfering with guest WiFi.

Maintenance and SLA

  • Permanent technical support, parts, labour, technician call-outs and consumables included in the OPEX model.
  • In the event of a critical breakdown that compromises operations: replacement of the robot with a new or courtesy unit at no additional cost.
